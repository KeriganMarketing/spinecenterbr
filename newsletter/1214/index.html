Lumbar Degenerative Disc Disease [Spine-Health.org]

- C. Chambliss Harrod, M.D.

Lumbar degenerative disc disease refers to the symptoms of lower back pain and/or radiating pain, numbness, or weakness in the legs stemming from a degenerated spinal disc. Disc degeneration is a natural part of the aging process and over time each person's discs will undergo changes, but not all people will actually develop symptoms.
